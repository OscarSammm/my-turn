WORK

Running

Running builds my endurance, mental strength, and consistency. It helps me stay in peak condition during and off season. Long runs improve my stamina, while sprints sharpen my explosiveness. It’s where I challenge myself when no one is watching.

Soccer

Soccer is more than a sport for me — it’s my identity and passion. I’ve competed at a high collegiate level, constantly pushing myself to improve technically and mentally. Playing as an attacking player has taught me creativity, discipline, and leadership. Every game is an opportunity to grow, compete, and represent where I come from.

Gym

The gym is where I build strength, power, and resilience. I focus on functional training that translates directly to performance on the field. Discipline in the weight room gives me confidence in competition. Every session is about becoming stronger than yesterday.

Jiu Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu teaches me patience, control, and strategic thinking. It challenges both my body and mind in completely different ways than soccer. I’ve learned how to stay calm under pressure and adapt quickly. It pushes me outside my comfort zone and makes me a more complete athlete..
